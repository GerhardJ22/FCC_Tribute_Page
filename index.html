pioneer of the personal computer era

Here's a time line of Socrates's life

  • 1955 - Born in Clifornia, U.S.
  • 1972 - He attended Reed College, but withdrawing that same year
  • 1974 - He travel through india to study buddhism
  • 1976 - He and Steve Wozniak develop and sell Wozniak's Apple I personal computer.
  • 1979 - Jobs saw potential on the mouse-driven and had a graphical user interface of the Xerox Alto
  • 1983 - Jobs developed apple lisa that inpired by the Xerox Alto, but it turn out unsuccessful.
  • 1984 - The breakthrough Macintosh, the first mass-produced computer with a GUI.
  • 1985 - The Macintosh launched the desktop publishing industry with the addition of the Apple LaserWriter. In the same year, Jobs departed from apple and took some Apple employees with him to found NeXT, a computer platform development company that specialized in computers for higher-education and business markets, serving as its CEO.
  • 1986 - Jobs helped develop the visual effects industry by funding the computer graphics division of Lucasfilm that eventually spun off independently as Pixar.
  • 1997 - Jobs returned to Apple as CEO and helped reviving Apple which is in the verge of bankrupcy.
  • 2003 - Jobs diagnose with a pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor.
  • 2011 - Dies at the age of 56.
